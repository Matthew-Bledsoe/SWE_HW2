### Example recipe to add into list manually
- Name: Chicken Teriyaki  
- Description: Juicy chicken cooked in a sweet and savory teriyaki sauce  
- Prep Time: 35 minutes  
- Main Protein: Chicken  
- Ingredients: 2 chicken breasts, 3 tbsp soy sauce, 2 tbsp honey, 1 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 clove garlic minced, 1 tsp grated ginger, 1 tbsp vegetable oil, 1 tsp sesame seeds  
- Steps: Mix soy sauce with honey and rice vinegar including garlic and ginger, Heat oil in a pan, Cook chicken until golden, Pour sauce over chicken, Sprinkle sesame seeds and serve
